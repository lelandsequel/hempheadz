# Hemp Headz SEO: The 5 Pillars (2025)

## 1. Technical Foundation
- **Mobile-First**: Fully responsive design.
- **Speed**: Optimized asset loading (Preload images).
- **Security**: HTTPS (via host).
- **Indexability**: `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml` configured.

## 2. On-Page Optimization
- **Keywords**: "Delta 8 Houston", "THCA Flower Houston", "Houston Hemp Shop".
- **Semantic HTML**: H1-H3 hierarchy updated for intent.
- **Meta-Data**: Unique titles and descriptions for AEO.

## 3. Local SEO
- **Schema**: `CannabisStore` JSON-LD implemented.
- **Geo-Targeting**: Focus on North Shepherd Dr, The Heights, and Garden Oaks.
- **Maps**: Optimized for Google Maps and Apple Maps Discovery.

## 4. Authority & Backlink Strategy
- **Niche Citations**: Listings in leafly, weedmaps, and Houston-specific directories.
- **Content Marketing**: "Be Someone" brand narrative for social sharing.

## 5. User Experience (UX)
- **Age Gate**: 21+ verification layer.
- **Accessibility**: ARIA labels and high-contrast glassmorphism.
- **Action-Oriented**: Clear "Visit Store" and "Call Us" CTAs.
